For custom shapes, you'll use the drawing tool:

  1. Tap each corner of your area on the canvas
  2. Tap the first point again (or hit Close) to finish the shape
  3. Enter the measurement for each wall segment
  4. Hit "Apply Dimensions" and you're done!
Tip: Start at the bottom-left corner and work clockwise. Don't worry about being pixel-perfect — the exact measurements you enter are what matter.

📖 Feature Guide

▸ Layout Tab
See your complete tile layout rendered in real-time. Use pinch to zoom, drag to pan. Tap Optimize to find the best pattern placement with minimal cuts and waste.
▸ Wall Dimensions
Enter your wall or floor measurements in inches. The app supports rectangular spaces with optional height/width variations for non-square walls.
▸ Tile Settings
Choose from preset tiles or enter custom dimensions. Toggle Nominal to enter box label sizes — grout is auto-subtracted. Supports hex, square, rectangle, herringbone, and mosaic patterns.
▸ Cutouts & Obstacles
Add niches, windows, outlets, or any obstacle. Enter the center position of the cutout from left/bottom edges, then the width and height. The layout automatically tiles around them.
▸ Cut Tracking PRO
See all your cuts measured for you! Tap any orange cut tile to see exact dimensions instantly. Mark each one done as you go — it turns red. Track your progress with the Full/Cuts/Done counter.
▸ Barcode Scanner PRO
Scan the barcode on any tile box to auto-populate tile specs. Saves time and reduces measurement errors.
▸ Project Sharing
Share your tile layout with friends, family, or your installer. Export and import project files to move between devices.
▸ Client Report Share PRO
Generate a professional, branded report to share with clients. Includes clean B&W layout visual, precise measurements, tile specs, material counts, and your company branding.
