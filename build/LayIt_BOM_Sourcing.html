📋 Sourcing Notes

  • Reliability priority: DAC and Op-Amp sourced from DigiKey/Mouser (authorized distributors) to avoid counterfeits.
  • TL072 warning: Heavily counterfeited on Amazon — only buy from DigiKey or Mouser.
  • Galvo set: Wonsung LK-20 is the most documented option in the DIY laser community. AliExpress bundle is $30 cheaper but less support.
  • Camera heat: OV5640 runs hot at 3.3V. Add a small heatsink or copper tape for continuous projection use.
  • Laser module: Laserland is the only reliable source for 520nm direct diode at this price. Most AliExpress "200mW green" modules are 532nm DPSS — avoid those.
  • Enclosure: Hammond is premium but their closest size is only 1.8" tall. The generic 150×100×80mm boxes are the right dimensions.
  • Order first (longest lead): Laser module from Laserland (2–3 weeks). Order galvo set from Amazon for faster shipping.
  • Misc total: The $22 line item assumes you already have some basic supplies. Full kits for everything listed under misc would run ~$80 but provide enough stock for multiple builds.
  • Bulk savings: At production volume (100+ units), expect 20–40% reduction across all components.
