## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Screen Recording
- Record on iPhone 15 Pro Max (6.7" for App Store 6.5" class)
- Use a pre-built project with hex tiles, one niche cutout, ~100 tiles
- Have a real tile box ready for the AI scan demo (or use a saved photo)
- Screen record with iOS built-in (Settings → Control Center → Screen Recording)

### Cursor / Tap Indicators
- Use a screen recording tool that shows tap locations (like ScreenFlow or Keynote export)
- Or add tap indicators in post: white circle that appears at each tap point and fades
- Keep cursor movements smooth and deliberate — no frantic swiping

### 11 Labs Voice
- Tone: confident, casual, knowledgeable. Not salesy.
- Pace: conversational. Not rushed. Let the visuals breathe.
- Suggested voice: "Adam" or "Antoni" on 11 Labs — clean male voice, no accent

### Music
- Soft electronic ambient. Think: Apple product video background music.
- Volume at 15-20% under voice. Drops to near-zero during key moments.
- Suggested: search "tech product demo background music" on Epidemic Sound or Artlist

### Editing
- Cut tight. No dead air. Every second shows something happening.
- Speed up typing animations (nobody wants to watch someone type "58")
- Slow down the AI scan result moment — that's the magic moment
- Slow down the cut detail view — that's the "wow, it tells me exactly where to cut" moment

### App Store Requirements
- iPhone screenshots: 6.5" (1284×2778) and 5.5" (1242×2208)
- App preview video: 15-30 seconds, same resolution, no letterboxing
- For the App Store preview, cut this script down to the 30-second highlights:
  scan tile → see layout → tap cut tile → see measurement → done
