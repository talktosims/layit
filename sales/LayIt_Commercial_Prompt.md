# LayIt Laser — AI Commercial Prompt Pack

## Instructions
Each scene below has a **Kling prompt** (paste into Kling AI) and a **narrator line** (generate with ElevenLabs or similar). Shoot for ~5 seconds per scene. Edit together with music afterward.

Recommended voice style: Calm, confident male voice. Think Mike Rowe meets a tool commercial. Not salesy — conversational authority.

Recommended music: Ambient electronic build, something clean and modern. Builds tension in scenes 1-3, resolves warm in scenes 5-7.

## Production Notes

### Kling Settings
- Use "Professional" or "Cinematic" mode if available
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- 5 seconds per clip (extend to 10 if Kling supports it, trim in edit)
- High quality / slow generation for best results

### Voiceover
- Generate with ElevenLabs, use a deep male voice preset
- Slightly slow pacing — let the visuals breathe
- Total narration: ~45 seconds

### Editing
- Cut together in CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or iMovie
- Add subtle transition fades between scenes
- Background music: search "cinematic ambient construction" or "modern tool commercial music"
- Total runtime target: ~50-60 seconds

### Alternative Kling Prompts (if any scene doesn't generate well)
- For the laser lines: try adding "neon green light beams" or "laser grid projection"
- For construction scenes: add "editorial photography style" for cleaner results
- If Kling struggles with the product: describe it as "small black electronic projector device" instead
