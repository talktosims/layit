LayIt
Laser-Guided Tile Layout System

Stop measuring twice. Stop cutting wrong. Let the laser show you exactly where every tile goes.

LayIt projects your entire tile layout directly onto the floor or wall in real time — every tile, every cut, every grout line. Visible. Accurate. Done.

The Problem

  • Layout by hand is slow — chalk lines, tape measures, dry fits
  • One misaligned row compounds across the entire floor
  • Complex patterns (herringbone, hex) are error-prone
  • Wasted tile from bad cuts costs real money
  • Experienced tilers are expensive and hard to find

LayIt Solves It

  • Green laser projects the full tile grid onto the surface
  • Shows exactly where each tile goes — including cuts
  • Camera auto-aligns to placed tiles for continuous accuracy
  • Handles any pattern: grid, brick, herringbone, hexagon
  • Less experienced crew members can lay tile accurately

How It Works

1

Design on the App

Enter room dimensions, pick your tile and pattern. The app calculates every tile and cut instantly.

2

Set Up the Laser

Place the unit on a tripod, point it at your surface. The camera locks onto walls or existing tiles automatically.

3

Lay Tile to the Lines

Follow the green laser lines projected on the floor. Each tile placed strengthens the alignment for the next.
