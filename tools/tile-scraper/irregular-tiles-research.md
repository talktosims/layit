# Irregular / Specialty Mosaic Tile Research

Research date: 2026-03-17

This document catalogs specialty mosaic tile shapes from major retailers with product IDs,
sheet dimensions, sub-tile dimensions, and materials. These shapes need custom rendering
logic in LayIt beyond the standard square/hex/penny/fishscale/diamond mosaics.
